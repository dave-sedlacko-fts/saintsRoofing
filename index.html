About Saints Roofing

Saints Roofing LLC is a locally owned and operated roofing company based in Poland, OH. We proudly serve homeowners and businesses across the greater Youngstown, Canton, and Akron areas in Northeast Ohio, as well as the Pittsburgh, PA region.

Whether you need a full roof replacement, storm damage repair, or routine maintenance, our experienced team delivers quality craftsmanship and dependable service on every project throughout the Mahoning Valley, Stark County, Summit County, and Western Pennsylvania.
